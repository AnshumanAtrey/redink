# Key Observations & Critical Risks

2-4 paragraph narrative on the most important findings — what an evaluator should walk away knowing. Distinct from a per-finding deep-dive: this is the editorial commentary on the *engagement as a whole*.

## Template

<from engagement-summary.md — Key Observations & Critical Risks section>

Typical content:

- The 2-3 highest-impact findings and what they mean for the org
- Chains of related findings that compound risk (e.g., outdated software + weak access controls = full host compromise)
- Systemic patterns (e.g., recurring weak credentials across multiple services)
- The riskiest paths an attacker could realistically take

## Notes

- Written by a human auditor; not generated by Claude. The orchestrator reads `engagement-summary.md` verbatim.
- This section is what senior management quotes back to the board. Keep it concrete, technical, and prioritised.
